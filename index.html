About

I am a Web Development graduate from Holy Angel University and currently work as a ServiceNow Developer. Beyond my technical role, I am deeply interested in expanding my skills in graphic and web design. Creativity has always been a driving force in my life—I enjoy expressing it through various forms of art, including drawing, painting, and diamond painting. I consider myself a lifelong learner, constantly seeking opportunities to grow, explore, and refine my craft.

Inspired by Jeon Jungkook of BTS, I believe that “living without passion is like being dead,” and I strive to bring that same passion into everything I create.
